This is my path from Recruitment to Software Development.

Before I moved to Germany in 2015 I had been working in IT recruitment for about 6 years.
Soon after the relocation I landed on maternity leave with 2 kids, one after another. It was a perfect moment to review my skills, re-check my goals and re-set my career objectives.
This is how I realized that I want to try myself in Web Development. And then my journey of self-education has started. I chose Web Development because I like to see what I am programming right now on the web-page.
On this blog I share the sources I've tried and can recommend.
